Worlds Beyond Number is a narrative-play, audio fiction multiverse created by and starring titans of tabletop Brennan Lee Mulligan, Erika Ishii, Aabria Iyengar, and Lou Wilson, designed by Taylor Moore at Fortunate Horse.

It is an independent, creator-owned podcast about playing games and making up stories with your friends. Across genres, across worlds, from immersive, years-long cinematic epics to wild, chaotic one-shots, the possibilities are… a lot.
